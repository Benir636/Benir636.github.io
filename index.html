Data Visualization - Final Project

by Renata Benigno

For my Data Visualization Final Project, I used a NYC Subway dataset. The date set included information about each subway station located in New York City, such as which subway lines pass through it, Annual Ridership and State of Good Repair, a jargon used by the MTA (Metropolitan Transportation Authority) to define which stations are in good conditions of repair and maintenance.

State of Good Repair = defined by the percentage of structural components located in a certain subway station that are currently in good conditions.

Here are some of the questions I explored with the dataset I had.

1) What was the Annual Ridership of each subway line, by borough, in 2014?

I was interested in exploring the data related to the stations' state of good repair, so first I created a map in QGIS, in order to visualize the data I had. I overlayed the original dataset with a shapefile of NYC Neighborhoods and with NYC Median Annual Household Income by Census Tract, and then I exported the map to CartoDB.
